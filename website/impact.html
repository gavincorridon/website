The problem came to be that because technology is one of the fastest growing industries, the laws that where made could not keep up with emmerging technologies. As a result, we saw alot of comapnies taking advantge of certain aspects of peoples data like facebook. Current Impact


Now a days, people are using the internet more than ever. Ecommerce is growing at rapid rates and at an all time high. Companies everyday are requiring more information from people because of the increased use of the internet. Companies look at the internet as a way to advertise to get more consumers to there products. The problem with this is that companies need ways to find out who to advertise to on the internet and how they should. Sadly, some companies trun to buying YOUR data from companies that you use all the time. This has a huge imapct on the privacy of our info and should be addressed immediatly with internet reform.
